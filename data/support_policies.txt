Support & Policies
==================

Customer Support
----------------
- Email: support@acmecorp.com (response within 4 hours for Pro, 1 hour for Enterprise)
- Live Chat: Available 9am-6pm PST Monday-Friday via in-app widget
- Community Forum: https://community.acmecorp.com
- Status Page: https://status.acmecorp.com

Support Tiers:
- Free: Community forum access only
- Pro: Email support, 4-hour response time
- Enterprise: Dedicated Slack channel, 1-hour response time, assigned CSM

Refund Policy
-------------
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. If you're not satisfied,
contact billing@acmecorp.com within 30 days of purchase for a full refund.

After 30 days, refunds are prorated based on remaining time in your billing cycle.

Data Privacy & Security
-----------------------
- SOC 2 Type II certified
- GDPR compliant
- Data encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3)
- No code or data is used to train AI models
- Data retention: Deleted accounts are purged within 30 days
- Data residency options: US, EU, and Asia-Pacific regions available for Enterprise

Uptime SLA
----------
- Free tier: Best effort (no SLA)
- Pro tier: 99.5% uptime SLA
- Enterprise tier: 99.99% uptime SLA with financial credits for downtime

Security Incidents:
In the event of a security incident affecting customer data, we commit to:
1. Notifying affected customers within 24 hours of confirmation
2. Providing a detailed incident report within 72 hours
3. Implementing remediation measures within 7 days

Acceptable Use Policy
---------------------
Users may not:
- Use our services to generate malicious code or exploits
- Attempt to reverse-engineer our AI models
- Exceed rate limits or abuse API endpoints
- Share account credentials outside their organization
- Use our services for spam or unsolicited communications

Violations may result in immediate account suspension.

Billing & Payments
------------------
- Accepted payment methods: Credit card (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), wire transfer (Enterprise)
- Billing cycle: Monthly or annual (annual gets 20% discount)
- Invoices available in dashboard under Settings > Billing
- Late payments: 7-day grace period, then service suspension
- Tax: Prices exclude applicable taxes; VAT/GST applied where required
