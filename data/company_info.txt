Company Overview
================

Acme Corporation was founded in 2015 by Sarah Chen and Marcus Williams in San Francisco, California.
The company specializes in building AI-powered developer tools that help engineering teams ship software faster.

Mission Statement
-----------------
"Our mission is to eliminate the repetitive, frustrating parts of software development so engineers
can focus on solving interesting problems and building products that matter."

Core Values
-----------
1. Developer Experience First - Every product decision starts with the developer in mind
2. Transparency - Open roadmaps, public changelogs, honest pricing
3. Speed - We iterate fast and ship weekly
4. Quality - Fast doesn't mean sloppy; we maintain rigorous testing standards

Company Stats (2025)
--------------------
- 250+ employees across 12 countries
- 50,000+ active developer users
- Series B funded ($45M raised total)
- Customers include Stripe, Vercel, Shopify, and Coinbase

Office Locations
----------------
- San Francisco, CA (HQ)
- London, UK
- Tokyo, Japan
- Remote-first for most engineering roles
